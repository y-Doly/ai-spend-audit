# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Target Audience

AI Spend Audit primarily targets:

- startups
- indie hackers
- small developer teams
- SaaS founders
- AI-heavy product teams

---

## Problem

Many startups subscribe to multiple AI tools without evaluating:
- overlapping functionality
- unused premium features
- inefficient pricing plans

This creates unnecessary recurring costs.

---

## Solution

AI Spend Audit helps teams:
- identify overspending
- compare plan usage
- estimate savings opportunities
- simplify AI software decisions
