# Economics

## Overview

AI Spend Audit focuses on identifying unnecessary recurring costs across AI subscriptions commonly used by startups and developer teams.

The product assumes that many teams overpay due to:
- overlapping functionality
- underutilized premium plans
- lack of pricing visibility

---

## Cost Optimization Model

The audit engine compares:
- current subscription plans
- number of users
- intended use cases

against lower-cost alternatives.

Potential savings are calculated monthly.

---

## Example Savings

| Tool | Example Optimization |
|---|---|
| ChatGPT | Team → Plus |
| Copilot | Business → Individual |
| Cursor | Business → Pro |
| Claude | Team → Pro |
| Gemini | Ultra → Pro |

## Business Opportunity

As AI adoption increases, recurring software costs also increase.

Startups increasingly require:
- pricing transparency
- usage optimization
- subscription rationalization

---

## Potential Revenue Models

### Freemium

Free basic audits with paid advanced analytics.

### SaaS Subscription

Monthly subscription for:
- dashboards
- tracking
- historical analysis
- team reporting

### Consulting

Offer manual AI stack optimization services for startups.

## Scalability Potential

Future integrations could include:

## Risks

- Rapid pricing changes
- Vendor API limitations
- Inaccurate usage assumptions
- Competitive optimization tools

## Long-Term Potential
